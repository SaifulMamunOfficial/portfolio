Saiful Islam Mamun is a Bangladeshi musician, blog writer, graphic designer, digital marketing manager and online activities. He regularly writes online, designs various interesting posters and banners.

At the same time, he regularly sings on private television in Bangladesh. He was born in his village Narayanpur village in Matlab upazila of Chandpur district. His father's name was Mizanur Rahman, and his mother's name was Maksuda Begum. They are 4 brothers and sisters. He is the second child of the parents and the eldest of the boys.

He is studying political science at Dhaka College, a reputed educational institution in Dhaka.
